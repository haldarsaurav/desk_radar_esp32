GC9A01 1.28″ Round LCD

240 × 240 IPS · 4‑wire SPI · GC9A01 controller
Round graphic display240²65K colours

A circular IPS LCD panel driven by the GC9A01 controller over a simple 4‑wire SPI bus. Full 240 × 240 resolution edge‑to‑edge in a round format, wide IPS viewing angles, and an LED backlight. In this project it's the entire user interface — every radar page, card and weather readout is painted here.
